Replacement

When replacement is active (ON), it means once an event occurs, it can happen again and all other events are NOT dependent on whether or not it occured.

hover on explanations to display large font.

Example: You draw a Queen of Hearts from a deck of cards. With replacement, you will replace the Queen back into the deck, in the forthcoming events, she could be drawn again, her being replaced means the probabilities will not change on the second event. Without replacement, she would be removed from future possiblities because she was not placed back into the deck. All further events are altered by her removed status.

Sample Space

Example: There are 4 red marbles, 3 green marbles and 10 blue marbles in a bag. The sample space would be along the lines of-- 4 red, 3 green, 10 blue, with a total of 17. A dice has 6 numbered sides, the sample space would be the numbers 1 - 6. A survey where the answer is , yes, no, maybe, 3 outcomes, total of 3. The sample space is the possible outcomes of the trial. Trial would be, roll of the dice, a selection of a marble, an answer from a survey.
